Mission Briefing — Read before you run
YOU ARE YOUR OWN TEAM.
Every loop you run gets recorded. Next loop, a ghost Echo replays your exact path beside you. Stack Echoes to cover every pressure plate at once — the door unlocks and you escape.

STEP 1
Scout the Level
When a level loads you enter Scouting Phase. The level is frozen — no timer, no danger. Study the layout: find the yellow pressure plates, the red turrets, and the green exit ◈. When ready, click Ready — Begin Loop.
STEP 2
Place Your Items
If your loadout has cards, you enter Setup Phase before the loop starts. Drag cards from your hand onto the grid — place Shields in front of turrets, Mirrors to redirect bullets, Launch Pads to cross walls. Scroll a placed item to rotate it. Click Start Loop when done.
STEP 3
Run & Record
The loop timer starts. Move with WASD. Every step you take is recorded. Walk over a pressure plate to hold it down — that position gets saved in your Echo. You don't need to win this loop; you just need to record a useful path. When the timer ends, your recording becomes an Echo.
STEP 4
Escape with Your Echo
Next loop your Echo ghost retraces your previous path. If your Echo stands on one plate and you stand on another, both are held simultaneously. Once all plates are active the door turns green — walk through to win!
⚠ Hazards to Know
⦿
Turret Bullet
Hits you = full level restart. Bullets bounce once off walls. Echoes absorb shots without dying — use them as shields.
▪
Void Tile
Purple-hatched floor. Step on it = instant death. No recovery. Walk around these.
❄
Ice / Sticky Floor
Ice = momentum builds, hard to stop. Orange sticky = crawl-slow. Echoes are unaffected by floor type.
